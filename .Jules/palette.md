## 2024-05-20 - [AuthModal Accessibility & Usability]
**Learning:** Adding a password visibility toggle significantly improves the experience for users who may have difficulty typing accurately (such as those with ADHD or dyslexia), and proper label-input associations are critical for both screen readers and click/tap targets.
**Action:** Always include a 'show password' toggle for login/register forms and ensure all inputs have `id` and `htmlFor` associations.
